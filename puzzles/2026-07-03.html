TWIN LANTERNS

Two lanterns, one wind — guide both home at once.

Moves 0  ·  Par –  ·  Best —

HOW TO PLAY

🏮 Every swipe, arrow key, or button press moves both lanterns one step in the same direction — they ride the same gust of wind.

🪨 Rocks and the garden edge stop a lantern while the other keeps drifting. That is your only way to split them apart. (Lanterns are spirits — they may pass through each other.)

✨ Rest the amber lantern on the amber ring and the jade lantern on the jade ring at the same time. Match par for a perfect night.
