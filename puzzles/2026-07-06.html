HOW TO PLAY

The engraved stone rim is fixed. The three brass rings inside it turn freely.

A spoke is a line of four numbers running from the rim to the hub. Make all eight spokes sum to exactly 20 at the same time.

Drag a ring to spin it, or use the ◀ ▶ buttons. Keys 1 / 2 / 3 pick a ring, ← → turn it. The badges around the rim show each spoke's current total.

Every 45° step counts as one turn. Par is 8.
