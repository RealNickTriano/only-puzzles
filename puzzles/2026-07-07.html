How to play

  • Drag a weight to a hook — or tap the weight, then tap a hook. Tap or drag a hung weight to lift it off.
  • A rod balances when the weight × distance totals match on both sides of its knot. The small italic numbers on each rod are the hook distances.
  • A hanging rod pulls on its hook with the total of everything below it. Rods and strings themselves weigh nothing.
  • Example: a 3 hung at distance 2 balances a 2 hung at distance 3, because 3×2 = 2×3.
  • Rods lean toward their heavy side, and knots turn gold when level. Hang all nine weights and level all five rods to win — a flawless solve uses 9 placements.
